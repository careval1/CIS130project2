About the Candidate

Ari Patrinos is a Northwest Philadelphia native with an impressive background, including a degree from Harvard and a master's in political science from the University of Chicago. He has experience on Wall Street as a stockbroker. Patrinos is running for City Controller to ensure Philadelphia delivers basic services such as clean streets and quality public schools.
